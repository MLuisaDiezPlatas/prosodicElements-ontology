Introduction back to ToC

This ontology contains the classes and properties necessary to structure the information of a prosodic analysis, that is to say, the information obtained from the study of the meters and the forms of versification. In poetry, the metric is the basic rhythmic structure of the verses of a poetic work.

Among others, in the ontology, the following classes are defined: Rhyme to represent the repetition of similar sounding words occurring at the end of lines in poems or songs, Pattern that is a basis class of a hierarchy of patterns like LinePattern, StanzaPattern and WorkPattern. All of these are designed to represent the information extracted from prosodic analysis using different unit of reference.
